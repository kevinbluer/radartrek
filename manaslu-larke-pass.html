Manaslu Larke Pass Trekking

Manaslu trekking considered as most challenging, untouched and scenic trekking trail in Midwestern part of Nepal which rewards you adventure journey with an unparalleled alpine experience. Manaslu circuit trek goes over the Larkya la Pass (5110m), offers you the cultural heritage, unrivaled beauty, biological diversity which was opened in 1992 for foreigners. Your trek goes through hilly region with magnificent Green farms land to Manaslu Conservation Area which takes an area of 1663 sq km, and also chance to see rare Snow Leopard, Himalayan Thar, and Musk deer. The core attraction of the trek is Green hills inhabited by Indo Aryan Hindus and middle hills by the Tibetian Tamang, Magar, Gurung and Ghale people.

Manaslu trekking also welcomes you with natural hot spring, Tibetan sacred Buddhist Monastries, alpine forests with vegeration, glacier lakes, snowcapped mountains, Buddhist Chorten, Praying flags, Praying wheel and unique architecture of tibetan houses. We start our trek with drive to Soti Khola from Kathmandu and start hike to Machha khola, Jagat, Samagaon, Samdo following Budhi Gandaki River. After crossing Larkya La, you will leave Manaslu and catch the Annapurna trekking trail descends to Besishar.

The eighth highest mountain in the world Manaslu (8163m), Ganesh Himal (7429m) and Lamjung Himal along with snowcapped high mountains makes you awesome in your trip. March to May and September to December is the best time to visit Manaslu where radar trek makes your trip unforgettable. Hurry up to see untouched landscape, hidden valley with adventure journey to Larkya La Pass.
