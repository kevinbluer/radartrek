Tamang Heritage Trail Trekking

Tamang Heritage Trail Trekking is newly opened trekking route in Langtang region located in cenral nothern part of Nepal. This is non tourist area which invites you to experience indigenous Tamang heritage, Buddhist way of living, natural beauties, rich architectural mountain houses, ancient monasteries, natural hot springs, wildlife activities, picturesque Gatlang village, snowcapped mountains, and beautiful landscape of alpine topography. It is a combine camping, home stay, and lodge trekkers package.

Tamang Heritage Trail Trekking welcomes you to explore Tamang culture and life style because Tamangs are mostly Tibetan people migrated from Tibet and Settled in Nepal. In Earlier days, Nepal government denied to give citizenship to this people so they changed their ethnicity to Tamang which they still write it. The culture, tradition, lifestyle, manners of these people are similar to Tibetan across the border.

The trail starts from Syabru Bensi, passes through Gatlang, Thuman, Timure, and Briddim after six hours drive from Kathmandu. Langtang Lirung, Ganesh Himal, Sanjen Himal,Tibetian Mountains and many snowcapped peaks can be seen from this trail. In the month of March and April, rhododendron blooms with different spices of trees, birds and butterflies. Those who are seeking for non touristic area, untouched heritage, traditional lifestyle of aboriginal Tamang people, Radar trek highly recommends and leads your footpath.
