Langtang Village Trekking

Langtang Valley Trekking is one of the nearest trekking destination from Kathmandu located in central north part of Nepal which combines the natural beauty and cultural diversity. Langtang is the first Himalayan National Park of Nepal which is inhabited by Tamang people, their culture and alpine scenery snowcapped mountains. This trek is occupied by beautiful mountain peaks such as Langtang (7234m) Langtang Lirung (7245m) Lakpa Dorje(6989m),Ganga LaPass (5122m)Sishapangma (8027m),Gyangchempo (6387m) , Langtang Glaciers, Langtang valley and Grassy land.

Langtang Valley Trekking begins after six hours drive from Kathmandu to Syabru Bensi through Trishuli River; ascend to Lama Hotel, Langtang village and Kyangin Gumpa. The valley offers pine forest, bamboo forests, rhododendron forest, rice terrace, swift mountain steams, snowcapped peaks, rugged ruck, and howling sound of migratory birds in lower part. Wildlife such as Red Panda, Monkeys, stunning view of glaciers, lakes,amazing photogenic sunset makes you awesome.

Langtang Valley Trekking gives you an opportunity to explore the Tamang heritage, their craftsmanship, language, dress, houses and their uniqueness. This trekking is very good alternative for those adventure trekkers who are willing to be out of the crowded area like Annapurna and Everest region.
